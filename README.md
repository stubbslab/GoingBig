# Going Big: A Scientist's Guide to Large Projects and Collaborations

**ISBN-23 978-0262547963**

**Author:** Christopher Stubbs

## About This Book

This book explores the unique challenges and strategies involved in planning, managing, and executing large-scale scientific projects. Drawing on experience from major scientific endeavors, it provides practical guidance for scientists, engineers, and project managers working on ambitious research programs.

## Download

The full book is available as a PDF: [GoingBigBook.pdf](GoingBigBook.pdf)

## Topics Covered

- Planning and scoping large scientific projects
- Team building and collaboration across institutions
- Managing complexity and technical risk
- Funding strategies for big science
- Leadership and decision-making at scale
- Lessons learned from major scientific programs
